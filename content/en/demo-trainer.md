::: pillars
eyebrow: How we train
title: Three pillars. Every week.
lede: A balanced week of work that builds strength, capacity, and resilience without burning you out. Programmed so the parts add up.
items:
  - title: Strength
    body: Slow, heavy lifts at progressive loads. Built around the squat, deadlift, press, and pull. Form first, weight second.
  - title: Conditioning
    body: Short, hard intervals that train your engine without crushing recovery. Mostly cyclical, some carrying.
  - title: Mobility
    body: A daily ten-minute routine plus a weekly long mobility session. The work that lets the other two work.
:::
