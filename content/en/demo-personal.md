::: prose
eyebrow: About
title: About the studio.
---
For ten years I have made still photographs of objects, rooms, and the people who care about them. The work tends to be quiet, the colour palette warm, and the deadlines reasonable.

The studio is a one-person operation. I shoot, I edit, I deliver — usually within two weeks of the session. For larger projects I bring in a second pair of hands; you will know about that on day one.

If your brand needs photographs that do not look like everyone else's, we should probably talk.
:::
